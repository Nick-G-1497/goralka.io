Haptically Controlled RC Car .

Designed and built an RC Car on a breadboard which could be controlled by having the user tilt their hand. This entailed a reversible motor driver to be controlled with PWM, a PID control system, as well as implementing bluetooth between two micro-controllers. Additionally, a handle was created in CAD and 3D printed
