Customer Churning Predictive Model (93%) .

Utilized serveral different algorithms to predict customers churning using machine learning. Each of these models was validated with cross validation techniques. The top performing algorithm ended up being a gradient boosted classifier (XGBoostClassifier) which yielded an acuracy of 93%. While this data can be used by banks in order to retain customers, this data can also be utilized by a regulating body such as the SEC in order to integrate into a larger model in order to identify preditory lending techniques and identify at risk consumers.
