Autonomous RC Car .

Utilized the Texas Instuments MSP432 microcontroller in order to construct an autonomous robotic car which could perform a series of challenges without any user imput. Challenges included dead reckoning, autonomous line following, and autonomously naviating a coridor without touching the walls. Implement many standard communication protocols (12C, SPI, CAN, UART) and utilized test driven and object oriented developement stategies. A link to working demo of line following is linked below.
