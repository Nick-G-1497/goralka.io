TryHackMe - Cybersecurity Training .

I subscribe to the TryHackMe.com cybersecurity training platform. On the platform I have learned the fundamentals of networking, cyrptography, malware analysis, and ethical hacking. I have completed numerous hands on lab exercises which applied the skills I have learned. One notable lab which I complete was utilizing the EternalBlue exploit to gain initial access to a virtual machine, then escalating myself to an admin user, and gaining full control. The EternalBlue exploit was developed originally by the NSA, but leaked onto the darkweb by the Shadow Brokers hacker group. EternalBlue exploits a vulnerability in the Server Message Block (SMB) protocol (CVE-2017-0144). In May 2017 the exploit was utilized in the WannaCry ransomware attacks which according to some estimates caused over $4 billion in damages.
