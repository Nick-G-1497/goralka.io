Radioactive Decay Modelling - Differential Equations .

The purpose of this lab is to to use numerical and analytical techniques to model and study radioactive decay chains. The MatLab software was used to evaluate equations and to create graphs in order to present the models in an accurate and concise manner. Primarily, the goal of this paper is to explore the mathematics describing such process and as such it will not focus on the physics and specific details of radioactive decay
