ArdenVent Volunteer Embedded Systems Developer .

Writing peripheral device drivers for sensors and actuators on the ArdenVent Project. The project is an international cohort of engineers volunteering their time to build devices to help address the COVID-19 crisis in areas with limited access to health care. All devices are designed to be operated in rugged rural access environments and are intended for the developing world but also are modern enough to be adopted in any state of the art ICU
